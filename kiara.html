Meet Sally the Chicken 🐔

Sally 🐔 was rescued along with her two babies from a factory farm where conditions were harsh and life was uncertain. Thanks to the dedicated efforts of Kiara's Haven, Sally and her chicks were provided with proper care and shelter.

Today, Sally and her babies 🐥 have found a loving home in a sanctuary where they roam freely, enjoying the sunshine ☀️ and a life full of happiness. Their story is a testament to the transformative power of compassion and care. 🌟

About Us

Welcome to Kiara's Haven, Asia's largest & leading digitalized animal adoption center! 🐾❤️

Founded by Mera and named after one of her beloved cats, Kiara's Haven is on a heartfelt mission to save and help every animal in need across the globe. With a commitment to leaving no one behind, Kiara's Haven endeavors to provide love, care, and support to every creature, ensuring a world where all animals can thrive. We believe every animal deserves a chance to live a happy and healthy life. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide the best care for our furry friends, ensuring they are ready for their forever homes. 🏠✨

With the support of our community, we have made significant strides in improving the lives of countless animals. Join us in our journey to make a positive impact. 🙏💖

Our Values

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a world where every animal can enjoy a life of dignity, comfort, and happiness. We strive to provide shelter, care, and advocacy for animals in need, ensuring they are treated with compassion and respect.

Learn More About Our Mission!🤍

Our Vision

Our vision is a future where all animals live free from suffering, where every companion animal has a loving home, and where wildlife thrives in their natural habitats. We aim to inspire compassion, promote responsible pet ownership, and advocate for animal welfare worldwide.
